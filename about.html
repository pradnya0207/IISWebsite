About Interface IT Solution

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate IT professionals, Interface IT Solution began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, practical IT education that prepares students for real-world challenges.

What started as a small training center in Baramati has now grown into one of the region's most respected IT institutes, with over 5,000 successful alumni working at top companies across India.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between industry and education by providing hands-on, project-based training that equips students with the skills employers actually need in today's job market.

Our Vision

To become the premier IT training destination in Baramati, recognized for producing job-ready, highly skilled professionals who contribute to the growing tech industry.
